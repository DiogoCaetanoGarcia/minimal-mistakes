Video compression


Geometric partition of blocks for motion estimation and compensation
The Group started researching several aspects of image and video compression, such as:
  • Encoding complexity
  • Wyner-Ziv coding
  • Scanned document compression
  • Intra prediction
  • Motion estimation
Furthermore, our work on non-rectangular block partition was later adopted by the AV1 open video coding standard.
